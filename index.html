About Us

REACH started in 2014 as a way to provide additional support to the student body from the people that know them best... fellow students! Each semester a group of six to eight students (known as REACHers) plan fun events and informative workshops. We also can act as a point of communication between the students and administration on heath and wellness issues.We are committed to supporting the community.. Think there's something else we could be doing? REACH out - we'd love to hear from you!

Want to Join?

If you want to get involved, then come along to our events! They are open to the whole student body, and it can be a great way to make friends with both freshmen and upper classmen. Stay up to date by liking our Facebook page, in most cases you can simply show up to an event but we will indicate where sign ups are required. If you'd like to be a part of the REACH core team then there will be an opportunity to apply. We advertise on Facebook and the Student Portal prior to each semester, and particularly consider students who regularly attend events.
