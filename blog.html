Self Care Tips

  • Self Care #1: Go to the beach. I know - you're busy, there are papers to write and exams to cram for, you don't have time for the beach right now. But think about how amazing the sand will feel on your bare feet, and won't you work harder if you give yourself a break?

  • Self Care #2: Write a poem, or a letter, or anything that comes to you. Writing creatively can be a great way to express feelings, or just have fun!

  • Self Care Sunday #3: Whilst we love social media (and appreciate the irony of posting this on Facebook), sometimes it's really not good for self-esteem. Take some technology free time to recharge your batteries.

  • Self Care #1: Go to the beach. I know - you're busy, there are papers to write and exams to cram for, you don't have time for the beach right now. But think about how amazing the sand will feel on your bare feet, and won't you work harder if you give yourself a break?

  • Self Care #2: Write a poem, or a letter, or anything that comes to you. Writing creatively can be a great way to express feelings, or just have fun!

  • Self Care Sunday #3: Whilst we love social media (and appreciate the irony of posting this on Facebook), sometimes it's really not good for self-esteem. Take some technology free time to recharge your batteries.

  • Self Care Sunday #4: The food you put into your body has a huge impact on how you feel. Whilst pizza is delicious, generally eating well is the 101 of looking after yourself.

  • Sunday Self Care #5: Stop comparing yourself to others. Whilst some competition is healthy, comparisons usually just make us feel worse. Focus on being the best version of yourself, and don't worry about what everyone else is doing.

  • Self Care Sunday #6: Surround yourself with people who make you feel loved, even when you're at your worst. Cherish the friends who lift you up, or simply sit beside you when times are tough.

  • Self Care Sunday #7: Enjoy the break, you've earned it.

  • Sunday Self Care #8: When school life gets busy and commitments take over our life, it can be hard to take a moment to simply be rather than our constant state of do. Find a quiet space, and appreciate the stillness.

  • Self Care Sunday #9: If you keep focusing on your weakness, then you're bound to feel worse. Instead focus on your strengths. Everyone has them: what are yours?

  • Self Care Sunday #10: Write a gratitude list! Taking the time to think about what you're grateful for is a sure fire mood booster.
