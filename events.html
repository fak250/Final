The Nook

The Nook

The Nook is a spafe space between counselling and friencs where you can share what's on your mind and can receive support. In room E238 from 8-10 pm on Sundays, you'll find two REACH members for you to come and chat. No one knows what it's like to be an NYUAD student better than the students themselves. Maybe there's something you've been struggling with but you wont someone more anonymous than a friend and more casual than counselling. Though we cannout be 'legally confidential,'we will adhere to safe space guidelines and will respect the privacy of what is shared in the room.

Consent Dialogue Series

Consent Dialogue Series

As part of Sexual Respect Awareness Week, the NYUAD Health Promotion Office, assisted by REACH, will be hosting consent dialogues. These are incredible sessions in which we discuss what consent is and how to have a healthy relationship. I personally believe it's one of the most important events we run, and would 100% recommend you attending. Simply choose whichever group you would feel most comfortable with and attend the respective session.
